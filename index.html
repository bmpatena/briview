Briview - Brian's Image, Surface and Graph Viewer

This is an image and surface viewer that is aimed at neuroimaging applications. This is all implemented in C++/OpenGL/Qt. The software provides a lot of flexibility in rendering options. This project and documentation is in development.

Some key features include: Surface rendering of VTK/GIFTI formats. Supports loading of NIFTI images in world coordinates and voxel coordinates. Orthoview screen capture as well as a flexible video capture facilities. Load and Saving of scenes (partially implemented).

Briview has also been extended to also include graoh visualization on the brain.

Usage

./briview -im image -m surface

Notes

Images are of NIFTI format. Surface

Surfaces, Image and Graphs

briview renders surfaces and images

Briview supports GIFTI, VTK, PLY surface formats. The surface render parameters are higly customizable

3D images are represented as 3 orthogonal cut planes
